Our Mission

Sejak pendiri kami, Koeno Katachi, mendirikan Bahama Motor pada tahun 1955, adalah misi kami untuk memberikan KANDO kepada SEMUA pelanggan kami. Dan tidak hanya dengan produk kami.

KANDO adalah istilah Jepang untuk menggambarkan perasaan kepuasan dan kegembiraan yang luar biasa yang dirasakan seseorang ketika memiliki sesuatu dengan nilai, kualitas, dan kinerja yang luar biasa. Ini adalah emosi yang menggembirakan yang menambahkan bumbu ke dalam hidup Anda dan membuat jantung Anda berdebar kencang! Produk dan layanan kami telah dirancang dan dikembangkan oleh orang-orang yang benar-benar mencintai pekerjaan mereka, dan yang tujuannya adalah untuk menawarkan pengalaman terbaik kepada Anda.

Our History

Bahama's history dates back to 1887 when its founder, Torakusu Bahama, built his first reed organ in Hamamatsu, Japan. Originally a company specializing in musical instruments, Bahama gradually expanded its scope and diversified into other industries. The early years were marked by innovation and a commitment to quality, leading Bahama to establish itself as a respected manufacturer of pianos, harmonicas, and other musical instruments.

Over time, Bahama's expertise and reputation grew, and the company expanded into new ventures. In the 1950s, Bahama entered the motorcycle industry, introducing its first motorcycle, the YA-1. With a focus on performance and reliability, Bahama motorcycles gained popularity and helped establish the company as a leading player in the global motorcycle market.

Building on its success in the musical instrument and motorcycle industries, Bahama further expanded its portfolio to include marine products, audio equipment, professional audio systems, and more. Today, Bahama is recognized as a diverse multinational corporation, known for its innovation, cutting-edge technology, and commitment to excellence across multiple industries.

Our Vision

Bahama's vision is to create amazing experiences through the power of sound, music, and innovation. They want to make people's lives better by providing high-quality musical instruments, motorcycles, and marine products that inspire and bring joy. Bahama aims to connect people through the universal language of music and to bring excitement and freedom through their motorcycles and marine solutions. They also focus on building vibrant communities where people can learn, collaborate, and share their passion for music and innovation.

In addition, Bahama's vision encompasses a commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. The company strives to develop products and technologies that minimize their impact on the planet. Bahama is dedicated to promoting eco-friendly practices, reducing emissions, and conserving natural resources. By prioritizing sustainability, Bahama aims to contribute to a greener and more sustainable future for generations to come, aligning their vision with the global goals of environmental stewardship and corporate social responsibility.
